Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

At MamaMinder, we believe that tracking your baby's growth, feeding schedules, and sleep patterns should be private and secure. This policy outlines how we handle your data with strict data sovereignty in mind.

1. Strict Data Sovereignty (Zero-Server Architecture)

MamaMinder is engineered to keep your nursery details and infant logs completely in your hands. We do not operate any middle-man backend databases that capture or process your baby logs:

  • Local Databases: All diaper logs, sleep sessions, dynamic feeding timers, and WHO growth milestones are stored locally on your device in secure, sandboxed storage.
  • Photos and Profiles: Your baby's name, profile pictures, and birthdates are kept purely on your physical device. We NEVER upload them to any remote servers.

2. Co-Parent Sync Operations

If you choose to connect and synchronize with a co-parent or partner:

  • The app utilizes direct peer-to-peer or secured channel connections to exchange logs.
  • These logs are transferred securely and are never permanently stored or cataloged on our systems.

3. Minimum Subscription & Diagnostic Metrics

To provide subscription services and ensure application stability, we interact with highly trusted third-party providers:

  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to verify your "Premium" status across devices. This uses an anonymous identifier and does not contain any baby names, logs, or personal information.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymous crash statistics and rendering metrics to keep the timers and timers service operating flawlessly in the background.

4. Security

We implement strict measures to protect your nursery logs:

  • Local Enclave Storage: Sensitive partner sync credentials and diagnostic tokens are stored within the iOS Keychain or secure Android sandbox.
  • High Contrast Safety: The app ensures all alerts and anomaly notifications are easily readable under low-light conditions without blasting your baby with high-intensity light.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page immediately.
