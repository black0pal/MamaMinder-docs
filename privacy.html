MamaMinder Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MamaMinder. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your family's data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the MamaMinder app.

2. Data Collection & Storage

MamaMinder is designed as a local-first application. This means:

  • All data you log (such as sleep schedules, feeding times, growth metrics, and notes) is stored directly on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health data or your child's data on our servers.
  • Your data remains entirely under your control.

3. Cloud Sync & External Services

In our current version, MamaMinder operates entirely offline. No data is synced to the cloud. If we introduce cloud-sync features in the future, it will be strictly opt-in and secured with enterprise-grade encryption.

4. App Analytics and Crash Reporting

To improve app stability, we use Sentry for crash reporting. If the app crashes, an anonymous report containing device information and the stack trace of the error is sent to help us fix the issue. This report does not contain any personal data or logs you have entered into the app.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Camera & Photos: We request these permissions solely to allow you to select profile pictures or attach photos to your notes. Images never leave your device.
  • FaceID/Biometrics: Used strictly to lock the app locally on your device. We do not have access to your biometric data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@blackopal.com.
