MamaMinder Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

How to Delete Your Data

MamaMinder stores all your tracking data (such as feeding, sleep, diaper logs, and photos) locally on your device. We do not store your data on our servers.

Option 1: Complete Deletion (Uninstall)

Because your data is stored locally, the simplest way to completely delete all data associated with MamaMinder is to uninstall the application from your device. Once uninstalled, all app data is permanently removed by the operating system.

Option 2: In-App Deletion

If you wish to clear your data while keeping the app installed:

  1. Open MamaMinder and navigate to Settings.
  2. Select Legal & Compliance.
  3. Tap on Data Deletion.
  4. Follow the prompts to clear all local application state and records.
