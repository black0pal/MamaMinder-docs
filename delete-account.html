Account & Log Purge Policy

At MamaMinder, you retain full ownership of your baby logs. Because our application is constructed on a privacy-first, client-side model, removing your baby data and clearing your active profiles is completed directly on your device.

How to completely wipe your data

1

Purge Baby Profiles: Open the MamaMinder application, go to the Baby settings screen, and select "Delete Profile" for each baby. This immediately Purges all local feeds, naps, diaper changes, and WHO growth milestones from your device sandbox.

2

Disconnect Partner Sync: If you enabled co-parent synchronicity, navigate to the Sync settings page and tap "Disconnect". This terminates all active secured channels and purges co-parent tokens from the iOS Keychain or Android Secure Enclave.

3

Cancel Active App Store Subscriptions: If you unlocked the premium tier, you must cancel the subscription through your Apple App Store settings. Since we do not host a middle-man backend dashboard, deleting the app from your device does not automatically cancel Apple Billing agreements.

Data Erasure Guarantee

Once you trigger a profile delete inside the application, your nursery logs are immediately overwritten and destroyed. We maintain no server backups, keeping your infant's information private and secure.
